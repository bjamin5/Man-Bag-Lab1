The Man-Bag

The Man-Bag, is a small, conveniently sized men's bag. It is great for your daily essentials (iPad mini, phone, keys, wallet, etc.) and everyday carry. The interior of this bag has one full-width zippered stash pocket. Made from heavy-duty Martexin American waxed-canvas with heavy-duty cotton webbing, this bag is guaranteed for life.
